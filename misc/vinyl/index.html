Regarding my newfound record hobby

Groovy Spinning Circles

I’ll start by saying that I can be a bit obsessive about my hobbies. When I say that I mean a few things:

  1. I could talk about them for hours on end (and sometimes do)
  2. I frequently think about what I want to do next (in other words, my hobbies usually grow in scale over time)
  3. I am a bit of an idealist when it comes to things I own (who doesn’t want their money’s and time’s worth?)

The first two points on that list have generally enabled me to find great satisfaction and achievement in my hobbies. But if the third one is significant, why in the world am I collecting records?
