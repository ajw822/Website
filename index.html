Welcome to my site!

I am a 2020 graduate of Northeastern University, with a combined major in Electrical and Computer Engineering. Since graduating, I have been working as an electrical engineer with Lockheed Martin’s Advanced Energy Storage division in Andover, MA, which is currently focused on the Gridstar Flow line of solutions.

In my free time I am an avid bicycle tourist, amateur bicycle mechanic, and all-around tinkerer. You can find me assembling guitars, practicing the saxophone, or 3D printing when I have the chance.
